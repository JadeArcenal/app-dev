# **Haikyuu!!** 
is an anime about *Shoyo Hinata*, is the main character that has incredible athletism who is inspired to play volleyball. He joins *Karasuno High* and teams up with *Tobio Kageyama*, his first rival but they became teammates. Together, they work to make their team the best.

## Why I Love Haikyuu!!
1. *Inspiring Characters* – Each Character has unique personalities.
2. *Good Storyline* – Great storyline in every characters and good character development.
3. *Great Animation* – High Class animation that will make you watch it again.

## Memorable Quote
"Even if we’re not confident, we’ll jump. That’s what it means to be a spiker." – **Shoyo Hinata**


> Haikyuu!! is an inspiring story of passion, friendship, and never giving up!
